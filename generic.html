Shipping Company Dashboard (Power BI)

Explore the Power BI Shipping Company Dashboard project, a comprehensive visualization of sales data for a shipping company. Leveraging Power BI and a star schema with a central fact table, this dashboard provides insights into sales performance through interactive elements, metrics, and visualizations. Enhance your understanding of the company's dynamics with a user-friendly interface. Download the Power BI file, open it in Power BI Desktop, and embark on a visual journey through sales overviews and customer insights. Your feedback is welcomed for continual improvement. Happy exploring!.

Delve into the Coffee Sales Data Analysis project, where I leverage Excel to unravel insights from coffee sales data. The dataset, sourced from Kaggle, undergoes manipulation, pivot table creation, and chart integration for a clear and visually appealing representation.
